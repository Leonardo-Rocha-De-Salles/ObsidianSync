# Successione Geometrica
$\{q^{n}\in N\} \quad q \in R$

$$\lim_{n\to+\infty} q^{n}=\begin{cases} 
+\infty & q>1 \\
1 & q = 1 \\
0 & -1 < q < 1 \\
\not \exists & q \leq -1
\end{cases}$$

# Successione Armonica

$\{n^{\alpha}\}_{n\in N} \qquad \alpha \in R$
$$\lim_{n\to+\infty} n^{\alpha}=\begin{cases} 
+\infty & \alpha>0 \\
1 & \alpha=0 \\
0 & \alpha<0 \\

\end{cases}$$
